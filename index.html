Give Back Memphis is an organization that provides pro bono consulting to locol nonprofits. They needed a web presence that could communicate their vision and process, but one that was also in keeping with their local scale and spirit.

After we determined that Wordpress was thier best option, I created a child theme. The coding on this project was rather light—mainly CSS and a little PHP—but I learned a lot about UX and iterative design by going through the process with this client.

WordFeast

WordFeast is my attempt to build something similar to GoodReads, but with an emphasis on personal utility rather than social sharing. As an avid reader, it gave me a chance to build something exciting while expanding my knowledge of ASP.NET, C#, SQL Server, HTML, CSS, Javascript and using APIs. You can check out my progress in these short videos, or take a look on github.
